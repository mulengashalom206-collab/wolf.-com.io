About Eureka Tech Zambia

Your trusted partner for premium HVAC solutions

Our Story

Founded with a vision to provide exceptional climate control solutions, Eureka Tech Zambia LMD has grown to become a leading HVAC service provider in Zambia. Our journey began with a simple commitment: to deliver reliable, efficient, and professional air conditioning services that exceed customer expectations.

Over the years, we've built a reputation for excellence through our attention to detail, skilled technicians, and unwavering dedication to customer satisfaction. We believe that everyone deserves a comfortable indoor environment, and we work tirelessly to make that a reality for our clients.

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide superior HVAC solutions that enhance comfort, improve energy efficiency, and create healthier indoor environments for residential, commercial, and industrial clients across Zambia. We achieve this through:

Quality Craftsmanship

Every installation, repair, and maintenance service is performed with precision and care.

Customer-Centric Approach

We listen to our clients' needs and tailor our services to provide optimal solutions.

Sustainable Solutions

We prioritize energy-efficient systems that reduce environmental impact and operating costs.
